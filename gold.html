Your Golden Journey

Day 1

Royal Srinagar Arrival

  • Arrive at Srinagar Airport.
  • Transfer to a luxury houseboat on Dal Lake.
  • Evening shikara ride with refreshments.
  • Overnight stay on the houseboat.
Day 2

Gulmarg Adventure

  • Drive to Gulmarg (52 km, 2 hours).
  • Gondola ride to Apharwat Peak.
  • Explore the meadows and enjoy skiing (optional).
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 3

Pahalgam Exploration

  • Drive to Pahalgam (96 km, 3 hours).
  • Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley.
  • Enjoy horse riding or a peaceful walk along the Lidder River.
  • Overnight stay in Pahalgam.
Day 4

Sonamarg - The Meadow of Gold

  • Drive to Sonamarg (80 km, 2.5 hours).
  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy snow activities.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 5

Srinagar Departure

  • Visit Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh).
  • Transfer to Srinagar Airport for departure.
