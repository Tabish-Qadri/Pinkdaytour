Your Platinum Journey

Day 1

Royal Srinagar Arrival

  • Arrive at Srinagar Airport.
  • Transfer to a luxury houseboat on Dal Lake.
  • Evening shikara ride with champagne.
  • Welcome dinner with live music.
  • Overnight stay on the houseboat.
Day 2

Gulmarg Adventure

  • Drive to Gulmarg (52 km, 2 hours).
  • Gondola ride to Apharwat Peak.
  • Private skiing session (optional).
  • Lunch at a luxury resort.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 3

Pahalgam Exploration

  • Drive to Pahalgam (96 km, 3 hours).
  • Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley.
  • Exclusive horse riding experience.
  • Overnight stay in a luxury resort.
Day 4

Sonamarg - The Meadow of Gold

  • Drive to Sonamarg (80 km, 2.5 hours).
  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy snow activities.
  • Private picnic lunch by the river.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 5

Yusmarg - The Hidden Gem

  • Drive to Yusmarg (47 km, 2 hours).
  • Explore the lush green meadows and Doodh Ganga River.
  • Enjoy a private bonfire dinner under the stars.
  • Overnight stay in a luxury camp.
Day 6

Doodhpathri - The Valley of Milk

  • Drive to Doodhpathri (42 km, 2 hours).
  • Explore the pristine meadows and natural springs.
  • Enjoy a luxury picnic lunch.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 7

Srinagar Departure

  • Visit Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh).
  • Transfer to Srinagar Airport for departure.
